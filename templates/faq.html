FAQ

Do you use AI?

No. All readings are written by hand.

How long does it take?

Written readings: 3 - 10 days depending on type. Live calls: next available slot.

What if I don’t know my exact birth time?

I can still read for you. I’ll explain limits and what we can do.

Do you predict exact events?

I focus on what is active in your life and how to work with it. I avoid exact timing promises.

Are refunds possible?

If I have not started the work, yes. After delivery, refunds are not offered.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is never shared with third parties.
